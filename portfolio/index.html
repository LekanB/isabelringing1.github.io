Vestal
Vestal is a buisness concept I pitched to investors at the 2016 Power Pitch Competition at the Illinois
Mathematics and Science Academy. The idea was a virtual-reality based social network where you could
interact with others using just a Google Cardboard Viewer and a smartphone.

In addition to a full-length investor pitch, I also had the opportunity to pitch my idea in a matter
of seconds to many people. An aid I used was a quick demo on a Galazy S5 that showed the capabilities of what
Virtual Reality and Vestal could do. A video of the demo is below.

ANIRA
A long time ago, when I was in middle school, I started to work on an interactive
visual game called ANIRA. The basic premise was that an old man searches for his wife
with the help of the girl he's babysitting, and ends up discovering some dark secrets
that drastically change his life. As of now, it's never quite been completed and I've moved
on to other things. But if you're curious, I've compiled a short demo reel of what I
had accomplished so far!
